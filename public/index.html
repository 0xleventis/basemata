How it works

      

        

          
01

          
Connect GitHub

          
Authorize with public_repo scope. Your identity attaches to every PR, building trust with maintainers.

        

        

          
02

          
Fill in metadata

          
Upload your logo, paste your contract address (auto-validated on-chain), and add socials and tags.

        

        

          
03

          
Choose registries

          
Select which DEX token lists to target. Each one maintains its own GitHub repo as the source of truth.

        

        

          
04

          
Auto-propagation

          
Once a maintainer merges your PR, the DEX frontend reads from that repo and your token appears automatically.
